About US

Fitness Defense is a dynamic sports company operating since 2002. We specialize in managing sports and recreational facilities in schools, universities, hotels, residential complexes, government entities, and sports centers. Our mission is to inspire an active lifestyle by offering enriching camps and training courses that promote physical activity, reduce screen dependency, and help individuals of all ages discover their passions.

We have proudly collaborated with leading sports institutions including Al Nassr Club, Al Hilal Club, and Mahd Academy. Through these partnerships, we support talent recruitment and development, fostering a path for athletes to achieve excellence.
